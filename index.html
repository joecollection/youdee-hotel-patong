Youdee Hotel Patong is an exceptional value-add acquisition opportunity in one of Southeast Asia's most durable leisure demand markets. The asset is priced at ฿350M — a 44% discount to replacement cost of ฿790–808M and below the market value of the land alone at current Patong CBD pricing of ฿365M/Rai. This fundamental undervaluation creates a structural margin of safety rarely available in Phuket's increasingly institutional hotel market.

The investment is a brand-conversion play with immediate, executable upside. The property has completed its 2025 renovation and rebranding from Mier Jarr to Youdee — the product upgrade cycle has begun. A franchise agreement with IHG (Holiday Inn Express), Wyndham (Tryp), or Accor (ibis Styles) would immediately unlock GDS distribution, loyalty occupancy premiums, and the ADR re-rating from ฿2,200 to ฿3,200–3,500 — directly comparable to the Holiday Inn and Courtyard Marriott properties adjacent to this site. At stabilised NOI of ฿28M and an institutional exit cap rate of 5.5–6.5%, the implied exit value ranges ฿750–950M on a Yr 3 horizon — delivering 2.3–2.8× MOIC and 20–24% IRR on Scenario B.

Guest reviews confirm the asset's two strongest attributes — location (9.2/10) and rooftop pool (9.4/10) — are genuine competitive moats already in place. Patong records the highest occupancy of any sub-market in Phuket (C9 Hotelworks 2026), and all three distribution indices (RGI, MPI, ARI) sit below 1.0, meaning a brand-affiliated operator would immediately capture disproportionate market share in a market with secular demand tailwinds from diversifying source markets, airport expansion, and institutional capital deployment into Thai hospitality.
