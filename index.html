08 · Investment Thesis Summary
The Compelling Case
for Acquisition
Structural Value Gap
At THB 350M (4.22M/key), the asset is priced at 44% of replacement cost and below the market value of the land alone. There is no scenario where an investor loses on intrinsic value — the land is the floor.
Operational Alpha Available
All STAR metrics (RGI, MPI, ARI) are below 1.0, meaning the hotel is losing market share it should be winning. This is an operational problem with an operational solution — revenue management, branding, and distribution can recapture this yield within 18–24 months.
Hard-Asset Downside Protection
Freehold Chanote title on 1-0-87 Rai of Hotel-zoned CBD Patong land. Market land prices in this precinct are 365 MB/Rai and rising. The acquisition price is effectively a land buy with a free hotel attached.
Brand Affiliation Optionality
The property profile — 83 keys, rooftop pool, CBD location, neighbouring Holiday Inn and Marriott — is a prime candidate for IHG, Marriott Tribute Portfolio, Accor MGallery, or Hilton Curio soft-brand affiliation, delivering immediate ADR lift and GDS distribution.
F&B & Ancillary Revenue Upside
The rooftop sky-pool and restaurant are materially under-monetised. A curated rooftop skybar with F&B activation targeting both hotel guests and walk-in Patong visitors can generate THB 8–15M in incremental annual revenue at minimal additional fixed cost.
Macro Demand Tailwinds
Phuket saw 8.8M passenger arrivals in 2025, with full-year 2025 hotel occupancy at 79.5% and ADR up 7.8% to THB 5,652. Ongoing visa exemptions for India, Russia, China, and Australia, combined with improving regional air connectivity, underpin sustained demand growth through 2028.
